About     Research

I'm a PhD student in Quantum Computing at the Quantic team of Inria and at ENS Paris.

My research focuses on quantum information. I study quantum error correcting codes to build an universal fault-tolerance quantum computer. I'm especially interested in local decoders of quantum codes and fault-tolerant logical gates.

Before starting my PhD I obtained a Master's degree in Engineering from Mines Paris, France, and participated in an exchange program at the University of Tokyo, Japan.
